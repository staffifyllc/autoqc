# PhotoQC Cold Outreach Playbook

Based on your existing email style (no em dashes, Level 3 personalization, breakup final step).

## Target Profile

**Who gets an email:**
- Real estate photography companies with 3+ photographers
- Active on Google Maps, Aryeo directory, or Instagram business accounts
- 50+ properties per month (inferred from listings or activity)

**Who does NOT get an email:**
- Solo photographers doing under 20 properties/month (unit economics break)
- Massive national chains like VHT Studios (enterprise sales cycle)
- Companies that already use a QC tool (rare, but check their about page)

## Personalization Data Points (Level 3)

For each prospect, find:
1. **Company specialty** - luxury? commercial? drones? - influences messaging
2. **Recent Instagram post or website update** - shows you researched
3. **Platform they use** - if Aryeo in footer, lean into API integration. If Dropbox based, lean into Spiro/Tonomo support.
4. **Team size clue** - "Meet the team" page, about us, Instagram followers

Use Apollo or Hunter.io to find owner email. Instagram DMs go to the person managing the account which is usually the owner for small/medium companies.

## Cold Outreach Rules

Following your established rules:
- Never use em dashes in emails
- Step 1 is personalization only (the first sentence is Claude-generated per-recipient, not template)
- Always end sequences with the "Should I stay or should I go" 1/2/3 breakup
- Complete the 4-email sequence in exactly 30 days (Day 0, 3, 7, 14)
- Use your real domain, real founder signature, never "The PhotoQC Team"
